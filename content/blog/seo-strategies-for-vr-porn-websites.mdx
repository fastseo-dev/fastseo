## 7. Frequently Asked Questions
**1. How can I optimize VR video pages for SEO?**
Use VideoObject schema, host videos on fast CDNs, include transcripts, optimize thumbnails (640×360 px), and add detailed captions with target keywords.


**2. Are AI-generated content tools safe for adult websites?**
Yes—when used for briefs and optimization, not mass generation. Always review and human-edit to ensure accuracy, originality, and compliance with E-E-A-T.


**3. What’s the best anchor text strategy for VR porn backlinks?**
Combine branded anchors (“YourSite VR”) with keyword-rich phrases (“VR porn website promotion”) and generic anchors (“click here”) to maintain natural link profiles.


**4. How do I handle age-verification and SEO?**
Implement server-side age gates using JavaScript that don’t block crawlers—provide a crawlable “splash” page with metadata and a link to the VR content.


**5. Which analytics metrics matter most?**
Track organic traffic to VR pages, average session duration (indicative of immersive engagement), Core Web Vitals, and conversion events (e.g., newsletter sign-ups, subscription trials).
