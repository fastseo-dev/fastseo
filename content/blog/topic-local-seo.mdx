#####  **WHAT IS LOCAL SEO?**
Local SEO, or short for local “search engine optimization’ is the process where your website or online shop undergo an optimization for it to be able to be searched right away in local search results. For example, customers may want to go out and chill in the nearest café. They may search “café near my place”, “café around my place” “good café to chill and hang out”. In this case, the business that came out on the local search result provided by the search engine the customer used. 

 But improving your local SEO is not that easy. It takes time and effort for you to able to get a good result. However, this is not a problem anymore, for various e-commerce companies offer local SEO services that will help you to obtain this.
##### **WHY IS LOCAL SEO IMPORTANT ?**
Local SEO is important for customers and people who usually search for a good product or service using search engines. A big percentage of this is through Google, Amazon, bing, and many more. People use their mobile phone, tablet or iPad to do a local search whether it is about their health, directions, place, people or a good movie to watch. That’s why optimizing your local business is significant for you.
##### **WHAT BENEFITS COULD YOU OR THE PEOPLE NEAR YOU GET THROUGH LOCAL SEO?**
With proper search engine optimization, you can be able to push your business at the top of the local searches, and this is a great benefit, for you won’t be needing to find your customers instead, people will come to find you. Local SEO also benefits not just you but as well as the people around you, where it is created for a convenient and easier to find a service and product around the area with just typing the keywords according to their needs. 

If you are offering to tutor for kids, parents in your neighborhood can just simply do a local search by typing on Google, “tutor for kids near me” and if you are one of the top searches, you can get more customers.
##### **WHAT ARE NEEDED FOR LOCAL SEO?**
In doing a local SEO, you just need to focus on the local keywords that customers might look for, depending on the service or product you are offering. If your business is a small dentistry clinic, you should include keywords like” “dentist near me”, “dentist in [place]”. You also have to focus on the local content, local image, and hyperlink optimization and other on-page SEO.
