## Why iGaming Guest Posting Still Works in 2025

Before the list: a note on why gambling guest posting remains one of the highest-ROI link acquisition strategies available to iGaming operators and affiliates.

The editorial backlink from a genuine gambling publication carries far more authority than any technical link manipulation strategy because Google cannot reliably devalue it — it is real editorial coverage, on a real site, with real traffic. iGaming backlinks from quality publications compound over time: a placement on a DA60 gambling media site today contributes to your domain's authority profile for years.

For industries like casino, sports betting, and poker — where paid advertising channels are either banned or severely restricted — organic search is often the only scalable acquisition channel. That makes the link equity from a quality iGaming guest post directly translatable into revenue.

The key differentiator between a successful gambling guest posting programme and one that produces no results is **publisher selection**. A link from a DA20 gambling blog with no organic traffic does essentially nothing. A placement on a DA55+ iGaming publication read by your target audience is worth dozens of the former.

## How to Evaluate Gambling Guest Post Sites

Before submitting guest posts to any gambling blog, assess these four factors:

**Domain Authority (DA) and Domain Rating (DR)** — use Ahrefs or Moz to verify. Target DA40+ for meaningful link equity. DA50+ is the sweet spot for competitive iGaming keywords.

**Organic Traffic** — a site with high DA but zero organic traffic has often been penalised or has an unnatural link profile. Always check organic traffic in Ahrefs or Semrush alongside DA. A site with DA45 and 20,000 monthly organic visitors is far more valuable than a DA65 with 300.

**Topical Relevance** — a gambling guest post on a genuinely gambling-focused site passes more relevance signal than the same post on a generic lifestyle blog with a single casino category. Prioritise sites where casino, betting, or gaming is a core editorial topic.

**Editorial Standards** — sites with genuine editorial review processes are the ones that produce link equity that withstands algorithm updates. Avoid sites that accept any submission with payment and no review.

## How to Pitch Gambling Guest Posts Effectively

Finding the site is only half the challenge. The pitch is where most iGaming guest posting campaigns fail.

**Personalise every outreach email.** Reference a specific article on the site. Explain exactly why your proposed topic serves their audience. Never send a mass template — gambling editors receive dozens of identical pitches daily and delete them immediately.

**Lead with the topic, not the link request.** Frame your pitch around the editorial value of the article you propose. "I'd like to contribute a piece on responsible gambling regulations in regulated European markets — given your coverage of UK/EU compliance, I think your readers would find it valuable" works. "I'd like to submit a guest post with a link to my casino" does not.

**Match the editorial level of the target site.** iGaming B2B publications require expert-level industry content. Casino affiliate sites want player-useful strategy or news content. Sending a player-focused piece to a B2B publication (or vice versa) signals that you have not read the site.

**Offer original data or insight.** Guest posts that include proprietary data, original research, or a novel angle that the site's editorial team could not produce themselves are significantly more likely to be accepted at high-authority publications.
