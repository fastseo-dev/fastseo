### **4. Link Insertion:**
Link insertion is a type of link building strategy that involves adding links to existing content on other websites. In this strategy, the link builder identifies relevant and high-traffic websites in their industry, and then reaches out to the site owners to request the addition of a link to their content. The link builder can offer the website owner a valuable piece of content, such as an infographic, that they can add to their site in exchange for a link.

Link insertion is a highly effective strategy because it allows the link builder to target websites that are already established and have a strong domain authority. This can help the link builder to increase their own website's authority and improve their search engine rankings.
### **5. Crowd Link Building:**
Crowd link building is a link building strategy that involves reaching out to a large group of people and asking them to link back to your website. This strategy typically involves creating high-quality content that is relevant and valuable to a large audience, and then promoting that content to a wide range of people, such as bloggers, journalists, and social media influencers.

The idea behind crowd link building is that by reaching out to a large group of people, you can create a buzz around your content and encourage people to share it with their followers. As more people share your content, it becomes more visible and more likely to attract links from other websites.

Crowd link building can be a highly effective strategy for businesses that are looking to build their brand and attract new customers. By creating valuable content that appeals to a wide audience, businesses can increase their visibility and attract links from a range of high-quality websites.
### **6. PBN Link Building:**
PBN (Private Blog Network) link building is a controversial link building strategy that involves creating a network of high-quality websites that are used to link back to a money site (a site that is designed to generate revenue). The idea behind PBN link building is to create a network of sites that are all linked to the money site, which can help to improve its search engine rankings.

PBN link building involves creating a network of websites that are typically owned by the same person or company. These sites are often created using expired domains that have a high domain authority and backlink profile. The owner of the PBN will then create content on these sites that is designed to link back to the money site, which can help to improve its search engine rankings.

PBN link building is a controversial strategy because it is seen as a violation of Google's guidelines on link building. Google considers PBNs to be a form of link scheme, and as such, they can result in penalties or even the de-indexing of the money site. However, some SEOs still use PBNs as a way to boost their search engine rankings, despite the risks involved.

It is important to note that PBN link building is a black hat SEO technique that is not recommended for businesses that are looking to build a sustainable and long-term online presence. Instead, businesses should focus on building high-quality links through legitimate and ethical link building strategies, such as guest blogging, broken link building, and link insertion.
